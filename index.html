Mission Statement

The New Guard is dedicated to the rigorous investigation of Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and parapsychological events. We bridge the gap between civilian observation and scientific analysis.

Field Research

Active monitoring and data collection of sightings and localized anomalies.

Archive

Accessing declassified documents and maintaining a database of verified reports.

Community

A collaborative hub for researchers, veterans, and civilian observers.
